How Cases Become Resolved
  1. Device telemetry or staff input creates an exception and opens a visible case.
  2. Caregiver follow-up is recorded as bedside support, medication confirmation, or a manual safety re-check.
  3. When the resident is stable again, the item leaves the urgent queue and moves into green resolved tracking.
